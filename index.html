Elon Musk's Life Journey

June 28, 1971 - born to engineer father and model mother
age 3 - drifts off as if deaf (he was not) so parents get his adenoids removed
reads 10 hours per day
age 8 - runs out of books to read at library, reads entire Encyclopedia Britannica
age 9 - parents divorce, chooses to live with father
gets bullied at school, thrown down a staircase
learn BASIC by finishing a 6-month course in 3 days
age 12 - writes video games Blastar, sells it for $500
age 14 - has existential crisis and reads The Hitchhiker's Guide to The Galaxy looking for an answer
decides on his life mission - to save humanity
father takes him to work and makes him do real work
gets Canadian citizenship through mother to eventually move to the US
age 17 - moves to Canada
works odd jobs, stays with relatives
gets a job shoveling dirt in boiler room for $18/hour
1989 (age 18) - starts college in Ontario
cold calls execs he reads about in the news asking to meet for lunch
top exec of bank responds and offers an internship
shows genius in banking but gets chewed out for using the exec coffee machine
sells computers out of dorm room
transfer to UPenn double major in physics and economics
1992 - runs a speakeasy from his house in college
1994 - gets Wharton economics degree (and later physics degree)
works two intern jobs: in ultracapacitors research and video games
brainstorms ideas for medical startup with brother
leaves Standford PhD after 2 days
1995 - starts Zip2 with brother, coding it himself
gets $28.000 from father to start with
1995 - sleeps in office first 3 months
1996 - raises first investment money ($3M)
gets pushed out from CEO to CTO by his investors
1999 - gets $22 Million form sale of Zip2
Mar 1999 - starts X.com--an oline bank (with his own money)
buys McLaren F1 for $1 Million--soon crashes it
competes for customers with another banking startup Confinity
Mar 2020 - merges with Confinity forming Paypal
argues with CTO over which operating system to use (PC or Linux)
2000 - marries college girlfriend Justine
Sep 2000 - gets ousted as CEO from Paypal (while on honeymoon)
moves to LA to be closer to the space industry
Oct 2001 - takes trips to Russia to buy a rocket to send to Mars
Feb 2002 - negotiations fail, realizes rockets are artificially overpriced
May 2002 - first son born
Jun 2002 - starts SpaceX to build his own rockets (with own money)
Jul 2002 - son dies from sudden infant death syndrome 10 weeks later
Oct 2002 - gets $250 Million from sale of Paypal ($180M after tax)
thinks about building electric cars
meets JB Straubel, electric car enthusiast
Jan 2004 - meets Tesla founders who pitch exactly what he wanted--electric car
Apr 2004 - invests in Telsa, suggesting JB and Tesla founders work together
2004 - twin sons are born
Mar 2006 - his first ever rocket launches and explodes
Jul 2006 - wins NASA contracts to demo cargo delivery to ISS and make Falcon-9
Aug 2006 - becomes first investor in SolarCity (cousins' company)
End of 2006 - triplet sons are born
Mar 2007 - 2nd launch fails
Mar 2008 - first Tesla customers get ther Roadsters at $109.000
Jun 2008 - divorces wife
Aug 2008 - 3rd launch fails destroying 4 satellites aboard including NASA's
Aug 2008 - SpaceX and Tesla are both running out of cash
Sep 2008 - 4th launch succeeds
Dec 2008 - wins NASA contract for 12 flights for $1.6B
cries realizing SpaceX just barely escaped bankruptcy
Dec 2008 - hours before Tesla would have gone bankrupt--raises funding
Mar 2009 - unveils the first Tesla Model S
Oct 2010 - buys a car factory for cheap from Toyota's failed joint venture
Jun 2010 - Tesla goes public at $17 per share to ^$24 by end of the day
marries Talulah Riley
2011 - promises to send humans to Mars by 2021-2031
Sep 2011 - announces reusable rockets (to make cost of space flight 100 times cheaper)
Feb 2012 - unveils Model X (SUV)
Mar 2012 - divorces Riley
May 2012 - Dragon docks with the International Space Station
Jun 2012 - starts shipping the model S at $75.000
Sep 2012 - starts trying to land dummy rocket Grasshoper vertically from small altitude
Sep 2012 - launches supercharger network (for long distance driving)
Jul 2013 - marries Riley again

Apr 2013 - attempts to soft land rocket from orbit on water
Apr 2014 - attempts to land rocket from orbit on a ship
Jun 2014 - starts building the Gigafactory to mass produce batteries

Sep 2015 - first customers get their Model X
